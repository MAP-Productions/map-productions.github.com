MAP Productions

MAP Productions was an interactive production studio based in Cambridge, MA from 2011-2013. Our creative, editorial and technology team worked with leading radio producers and filmmakers around the world to create web documentaries that span the globe and uncover new perspectives on the human experience. Our work has been recognized internationally, from Webbys (nominimated for Best Music Site in 2013) to the IDFA DocLab to being showcased at the FCC in Washington D.C. as examples of the future of media.
